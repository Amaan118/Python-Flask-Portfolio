Hangman

In this game we first have to chose a difficulty from Amateur, Medium and Advanced. Based on our chosen difficulty lives are assigned. After that the system choses any random word from a document of 2465 words. Player has to guess the letter correctly before the life runs out. If player fails he/she will hanged or else he/she will be saved. After every round your result is stored in Hangman_Data file as a record. If you find any difficulties you can check out the help section.
